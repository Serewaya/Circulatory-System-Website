Atherosclerosis

Atherosclerosis is a disease that occurs when the blood vessels that carry all of your nutrients and oxygen to the heart. This disease is known as the hardening of the arteries because the stiffness of the vessel occurs when plaque builds up and eventually block the arteries from delivering the nutrients and oxygen to your heart. This is bad because of course you need oxygen to breathe and you need nutrients to stay healthy and alive.

Atherosclerosis is actually the number one killer of Americans either by heart attacks or blood clots.

How to prevent Atherosclerosis:

The main way to help with Atherosclerosis is actually to go on a diet or update your current one. This is used to lower your blood pressure. The best foods to clean out are-

1. Asparagus is actually one of if not the best food for clearing plaque out of your arteries.

2. Avocado helps reduce bad cholesterol and increase the amount of good cholesterol which helps clean your arteries.
