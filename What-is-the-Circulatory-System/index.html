What is the circulatory system?

The Circulatory System

The Circulatory System also known as the Cardiovascular System is the system which is responsible for carrying proteins and other nutrients. It ensures that every organ gets all of the things that they need to operate, such as nutrient rich blood and special proteins. The Circulatory System also runs throughout the body with the heart at the forefront.

The Heart

The heart is the main organ that the circulatory system depends on. It is made of specialized cardiac muscle tissue that allows it to act as a pump within the circulatory system.

Parts of the Heart

The human heart is divided into four chambers. There are one atrium and one ventricle on each side of the heart, the atria receive blood and the ventricles pump blood. This function is essential for the operation of the Circulatory System.
